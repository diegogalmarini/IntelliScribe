## How to use it

1.  Navigate to the **"Record"** section on your Dashboard.
2.  Select **"Microphone"** mode.
3.  Choose your input device if you have multiple microphones connected.
4.  Click the large circular button to **Start Recording**.
5.  You can **Pause** and **Resume** at any time.
6.  When finished, click **Stop**. The recording will process automatically.

> **Note:** Recording in quiet environments drastically improves transcription accuracy and speaker identification.
