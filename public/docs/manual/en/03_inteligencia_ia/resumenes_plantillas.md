
# AI Summary Templates

**Intelligence Templates** are the structured brain of Diktalo. They don't just summarize; they **transform** your recordings into knowledge assets ready to be shared, archived, or executed.

Each template trains the AI with a specific "role" (e.g., Lawyer, Project Manager, Journalist) to extract exactly what you need.

## Intelligence Catalog

### 1. üìù Adaptive Summary (Autopilot)
**The gold standard for 80% of your recordings.**
This template uses context algorithms to automatically detect the type of conversation and adjust the summary structure on the fly.

**Ideal Use Cases:**
- **Personal voice notes:** Converts your scattered thoughts into ordered lists.
- **Informal meetings:** Captures what matters without needing a prior agenda.
- **General use:** When you're not sure which template to choose, this is the safest option.

**What you get (Output):**
A balanced document with key points, a narrative synthesis, and the most important bullets.

### 2. üéØ Logical Reasoning (Reasoning Engine)
**Designed to dissect complexity.**
More than a summary, it is a logical audit of the conversation. This template tracks the chain of arguments, counter-arguments, and the evolution of ideas until reaching a conclusion.

**Ideal Use Cases:**
- **Strategic debates:** Understand how and why a difficult decision was made.
- **Conflict resolution:** Map the stances of each party.
- **Technical brainstorming:** Filter viable ideas from discarded ones.

**What you get (Output):**
```
CONTEXT: [The initial problem]
ARGUMENT ANALYSIS:
  - Stance A (John): [Arguments in favor]
  - Stance B (Mary): [Risks pointed out]
EVOLUTION: [How opinion changed during the talk]
FINAL CONCLUSION: [The consensus reached]
```

### 3. üìä Structured Minutes (Detailed Report)
**The ultimate tool for corporate documentation.**
Generates an exhaustive and granular report. Nothing is left out. Everything is meticulously categorized by topics, subtopics, and speakers.

**Ideal Use Cases:**
- **Board Meetings:** Where every detail must be recorded.
- **Training Workshops:** To create subsequent study material.
- **Research Interviews:** Extract insights without losing nuance.

**What you get (Output):**
A deep hierarchy of TOPIC > SUBTOPIC > DETAIL, along with an index of people, dates mentioned, and key metrics detected.

---

### 4. üìã Verbatim Transcript (Clean Text)
**Word-for-word forensic record.**
This mode disables synthesis algorithms to deliver a pure, faithful, and complete transcript. It eliminates noise (fillers, stutters) but retains 100% of the original content.

**Ideal Use Cases:**
- **Regulatory Compliance:** Unalterable legal archive.
- **Subtitling:** Perfect base for creating video scripts (SRT).
- **Journalistic Quotes:** When you need the exact phrase, not an interpretation.

**What you get (Output):**
Running text, clean and professionally punctuated, ready to copy and paste into legal documents or articles.

---

### 5. üíº Executive Summary (Executive Brief)
**Radical clarity for busy leaders.**
The "TL;DR" (Too Long; Didn't Read) template. Condenses hours of conversation into the 3 points that really matter for decision-making.

**Ideal Use Cases:**
- **Reports to C-Level:** Quick updates for executives.
- **Project Handover:** What the next team needs to know.
- **Fast Reading:** To review meeting content in 30 seconds.

**What you get (Output):**
Three high-impact blocks: THE SITUATION, THE DECISION, and THE REQUIRED ACTION. Maximum 200 words.

## How to Boost Your Results

1.  **Clear Audio = Smart AI:** Although Diktalo is powerful, audio with less echo improves speaker identification accuracy.
2.  **Mention "Actions":** If you explicitly say *"We agreed that John sends the email on Tuesday"*, the AI will detect it as a Task with date and owner automatically.
3.  **Experiment:** Don't be afraid to process the same recording with two different templates (e.g., "Executive" for your boss and "Detailed" for your archive) using the **Regenerate** function.
